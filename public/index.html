Food Materials

Printable Food Ingredients

We work with your formulation — or develop one. Any pumpable food material can be precisely deposited: chocolate, sugar, puree, gel, or paste. From 0.5 mm micro-garnishes to full plated constructs.

Allergen-free, dietary-compliant, and nutritionally calibrated formulations available. FDA food-contact materials throughout.

Applications

Food Printing Across Industries

Haute Cuisine & Fine Dining

Precision edible garnishes, architectural sauce geometries, and multi-layer plating elements that manual plating cannot repeat across a full restaurant service.

Personalized Nutrition

Nutritionally calibrated food products for clinical and wellness applications — dysphagia-friendly textures with precise macro and micronutrient dosing.

Confectionery & Chocolate

Tempered chocolate prints with precision snap and gloss. Isomalt sugar structures in clear, opaque, and multi-color options for pastry and confectionery applications.

Food R&D & Prototyping

Rapidly prototype new food product concepts — test texture combinations, flavor delivery geometries, and novel ingredient pairings at small scale before production.
